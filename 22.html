Size

   01| pickUp S
   **| jmp01:
   02| if W == "nothing" and
   **|    SW == "nothing" and
   **|    S == "nothing" and
   **|    E == "a wall" or
   **|    W > "my item" or
   **|    E > "my item" or
   **|    N == "a worker"
   03|| step S
   04| step E
   05| jump jmp01

Speed

   01| step S
   02| step S
   03| pickUp N
   **| jmp01:
   04| if W > "my item" or
   **|    E > "my item" or 
   **|    "my item" < "90"
   05|| step S
   06| step E
   07| step E
   08| jump jmp01
Both the size and speed solutions are considered "hacks," as they do not produce the correct solution in all cases; they are just likely to produce the best solution. In the speed case, the number "90" may need to be adjusted in combination with getting lucky test data.
