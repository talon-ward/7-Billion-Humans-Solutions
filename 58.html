The size solution comes from the realization that stripes work and that the program won't end if workers keep picking up datacubes, so the E and W (or N and S or NE and SW or NW and SE) options are removed from the "if" statement, which wraps the "pickUp" command. The speed solution comes from isolating workers and telling them exactly what to do. It is certainly not the optimal solution, but I opted for greater code reuse, since it certainly is good enough to beat the 20 secs goal.
