The size solution is just a little tricky but follows from pretty standard optimizations once you get the right setup. The speed solution, however, is a complete hack. It requires numerous failed attemps and lucky test data to get at least 50% right at 11 secs (the speed is always 11, but there is a high probability of failure). A guaranteed solution based on the same setup as the size solution with various and inventive optimizations can complete in 14 secs.
