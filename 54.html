  The key insight in both solutions is that you don't have to actually compute the average, since the program will automatically stop when all the datacubes are the right number. The speed solution is a "hack" that lets larger averages (with much longer runtimes) time out, so only the shorter, successful runtimes count toward the average. The speed result are highly variable and may be able to get even lower with more runs and lucky test data.
