Solution

   **| jmp01:
   01| step W, E
   02| if SW == "a datacube" and
   **|    S == "a datacube" and
   **|    SW > S and
   **|    W != "a worker"
   03|| pickUp SW
   04|| drop
   05|| step SW
   06|| pickUp E
   07|| drop
   08|| pickUp NE
   09|| step E
   10|| drop
   11|| step N
   12| jump jmp01
The speed is highly variable, so run it a few times for a good time. There is a bit of subtlety involved: the "step N" on line 11 is very important. If you use "step NW" or "step NE" instead, you will get terrible times, since you bias the position of your worker. The expectation should be zero net horizontal movement.
