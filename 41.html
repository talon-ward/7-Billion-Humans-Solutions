The size solution is just straightforward counting. The speed solution is incredibly deceptive, however, since the directions specifically state that "all workers must then exit the room by stepping into one of the conveniently placed infinite pit holes." In fact, they just need to exit the room, not necessarily through a hole. This can be accomplished by dividing by zero, which causes a worker to self-destruct.
