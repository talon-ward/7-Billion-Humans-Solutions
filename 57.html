The size challenge is somewhat "hacky" in that it sometimes times out, but it's the obvious choice. The speed challenge is isolating the workers and explicitly telling each one what to do. It's a little tricky in that the paths of the workers are determined by the geometry of the board, but it's fairly obvious once you count the number of squares and divide by the number of workers to find out a worker should complete at most six datacubes.
