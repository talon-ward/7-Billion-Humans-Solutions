Apparently the size challenge is a bit sneaky, but where you need to go depends only on the difference between the next datacube and the current datacube. Of course, a hole counts as a zero when taking the difference. For the speed challenge, the bottleneck is obviously the group of workers who are farthest away. These workers are isolated and their paths prescribed, while the remaining workers simply use a generic algorithm. The distant workers are broken into two groups to keep the hole in use as much as possible.
